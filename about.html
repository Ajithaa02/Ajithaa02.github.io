About Us

At Vigilate, we believe that a watch is more than just a timekeeping device—it's a personal expression of style, a symbol of craftsmanship, and a companion for life’s moments. Founded with a passion for precision and an eye for timeless design, we are dedicated to creating exceptional watches that combine elegance, durability, and functionality.

Every piece we create is crafted with meticulous attention to detail, using only the finest materials, such as stainless steel, sapphire crystal, and genuine leather, to ensure longevity and style.

With a blend of traditional watchmaking techniques and innovative technology, we strive to deliver watches that not only look remarkable but also withstand the test of time. We are passionate about our craft and take pride in the trust our customers place in us, whether they’re buying their first watch or adding to a cherished collection.

Our Services

Service Center Locator

Every official Vigilate store and Vigilate affliated is authorized to recieve your Vigilate watch for servicing.

Regular Servicing

Vigilate watches benefit from regular servicing by a professional, typically every 3-5 years, depending on the brand and model. During a service, a certified watchmaker will inspect, clean, and lubricate the internal mechanisms to ensure optimal performance and accuracy.

Water Resistance Check

If your watch is water-resistant, have its seals and gaskets checked and replaced every few years to maintain water resistance. Avoid exposing leather straps to water, as this can damage the material.
